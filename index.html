Abstract

The subject is a psychologist (MSc; age = 27), researcher, UX student, IT technician with background in Front-end Development and a creative (music, lyrics, videos, graphics etc.) currently looking for a job.

Research shows he loves empathizing with others and gathering insights to better help people and create what's truly desirable to them. He seems to have great analytical skills and be good at com­plex problem solving. Communication, research, learning and presentation skills appear to be his additional strengths.

He wrote psychological and pedagogical papers, coded his own web­sites for conducting research at the best possible level, worked in schools and variety of different places with various specialists and goals as a salesman, IT technician, psychologist, counselor, freelance: teacher, graphic designer, musician, data analyst and more.

Daniel is very passionate about UX and combining the wide arrangement of skills and passions he's acquired. The insights explicitly picture him as someone who'd add value to our team.

Keywords: psychologist, researcher, ux freak, creative, front-end dev
